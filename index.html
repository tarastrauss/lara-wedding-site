How We Met

Some time ago, in an apartment not so far away... Leah was about to give up on the dating game when she came across a profile on jSwipe. Intrigued by what she saw, she changed her mind and decided to reach out. Several months and about a dozen unanswered messages later, she finally saw a reply!

Meanwhile, Tara casually logged in to her jSwipe profile one day, not expecting anything exciting. She saw several messages from a person named "El". Impressed by the questions El had asked Tara about her profile, Tara quickly responded.

The two started corresponding on jSwipe, soon moving to text, then to e-mail and finally to the phone. Not even a day later, they met on the morning of April 7th and have barely spent time apart since.

The Proposal

After months of Leah teasing Tara about "when are we gonna get mawwwweeeed", Tara planned an extra special day. Just before midnight on May 3rd, Tara pointed out that it was almost Star Wars Day. She suggested that they sit down to play one of Leah's favorite Star Wars games - Knights of the Old Republic.

Tara played the first level of the game, pretending she didn't know how to use the controls. When she got to the end of the intro level, she told Leah she was too scared to go any further, and asked Leah to take over.

Much to Leah's surprise, the characters in the game started talking in rhyme about things that have nothing to do with Star Wars. At first, Leah thought it was a joke. It wasn't until one of the characters mentioned a shiny ring, that she caught on to what was happening.

The characters told Leah to go to the spot in the game "where she can hear music". When Leah found the game's Cantina, Savage Garden's "I Knew I Loved You Before I Met You" started playing in the game. Tara got down on one knee and proposed in butchered, rhyming hebrew.

And she said yes!!!!
